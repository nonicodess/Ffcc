History and Mission

In the wake of a fire or disaster, people are injured, homes and families are destroyed and lives changed forever. The firefighters charitable foundation (FFCF) know first hand the pain and suffering that can be caused in an instant. Since 1991 our not for profit 501 (c) 3 organization provides direct aid to victims of fire or disaster, other non-profit agencies, and volunteer fire departments. Our primary focus is to provide much needed financial assistance to individuals who have been affected by a fire or disaster, to support other disaster or prevention related charities and institutions, and volunteer fire departments. FFCF also has a nationwide volunteer brigade that acts as our eyes and ears to identify those in critical need. Our support services and charitable outreaches include but are not limited to the following

Individuals

FCF directs financial aid to individuals who have suffered damage to a fire or disaster to cover costs associated with
  • Medical Care/ Rehabilitation
  • Housing
  • Food, Shelter, Clothing
  • Medical Supplies and Equipment
  • Educational/ Training Scholarships

Volunteer Fire Departments

  • Equipment
  • Supplies
  • Maintenance
  • Repairs
  • Educational and Community Outreach Programs
  • Aid to Fire Victims
Institutions/ Charities FCF provides funding for:
  • Educational programs on fire safety
  • Children's Burn Camps and Burn Hospitals
  • Organizations providing assistance to fire or disaster victims
  • Life saving equipment and supplies
  • Establishment of Juvenile Fire Setters Programs
  • Support of charitable related outreach programs.
